Reporter Redesign

What is the Reporter?

The Reporter is a student run magazine at RIT. The design has many issues. Among them are the design is too cluttered, hierarchy is boring and plain, Story labels are have information separated, tweet bar is far to big and prevalent and serves no purpose. The goal is get people interested in reading more of the articles on the Reporter while making a UI that appeals to more of the target audience (that being people in there 20’s). The solution is to use high quality photography instead of illustrations to keep a constant feel and look while making the design less uniform so that most of the stories don’t blend in with each other. Have more stories from other kinds of news for people skimming thought that website with large typography to grab attention
