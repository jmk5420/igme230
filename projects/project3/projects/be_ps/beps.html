Be Playstation Campain

What is Be Playstation

This is an ad campaign that I made for the PlayStation brand. It was inspired by simple icons that have been used to represent more complicated characters and to give them new context. My goal was to recreate the characters of PlayStation in this style. I chose not to give these characters eyes because eyes are the most expressive part of the human face, so finding ways to represent these characters without using the eyes was the challenge of this project. The use of these icons would be for merchandising as well as animated ads. These ads are meant to be simple and cute to appeal to a younger audience. The end goal is to have one ad for each of the characters with a two word expression representing that character and are then followed be the phrase “Be PlayStation”.
