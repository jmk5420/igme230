New Media Bumper

What is the New Media Bumper?

This was a project where we were asked to create a bumper to be played at the begining of all the videos of the New Media Design Vimeo chanel

My Idea

My idea was to make the piece about creativity and not technology. I accomplished this by making the New Media logo be surrounded by abstract imagery that is meant to be representative of the idea the fly around in one's head when they are creative
